ELENA SEDINA
(born Jun-01-1968, 52 years old) Ukraine (federation/nationality Italy)
[what is this?]
WGM/IM Elena Sedina was born in Kiev and learned the rules of chess at the age of 7. She was awarded the WIM title in 1991, the WGM title in 1996 and the IM title in 1998. She moved to Italy in 1995 and palys for the Italian Chess Federation since April 2001.

In 2005 she was the first woman ever to win the Australian 2005 Open Championship.
