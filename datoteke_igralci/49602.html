NIKOLA SEDLAK
(born Dec-13-1983, 36 years old) Yugoslavia (federation/nationality Serbia)
[what is this?]
FM (1998); IM (2000); GM (2003); Olympiad gold medalist (2014).

GM Sedlak won the 3rd European Union Individual Chess Championship in 2007. Winner of the International Chess Festival BH Telecom (Sarajevo) 2009. Won the Serbian Championship in 2010.

Won individual gold for board 4 for Serbia at the Chess Olympiad (2014) in Tromsø.
