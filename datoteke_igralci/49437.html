KAMIL MITON
(born Apr-12-1984, 36 years old) Poland
[what is this?]
Grandmaster. Won Cannes in 2000 and Bajade de la Virgen in 2005. Winner of 33rd World Open: Open Section (2005). Runner-up in the 2007 Canadian Open. He won the Ciudad de La Laguna (Tenerife, Spain) 5-11 April 2010 with the same score as Lazaro Bruzon, Bojan Kurajica, Yuri Gonzalez Vidal, Evgeny Gleizerov and Bartlomiej Heberla.
