JON LOFTUR ARNASON
(born Nov-13-1960, 59 years old) Iceland
[what is this?]

Jón Loftur Árnason was awarded the IM title in 1979 and became a GM in 1986. He was World Under 16 Champion in 1977 and Icelandic champion in the same year. He won the national championship again in 1982 and 1988. He also won 1st prizes at Husavik 1985 and Plovdiv 1986 and tied for 1st at Helsinki 1986.
