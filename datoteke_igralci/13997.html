ALEKSA STRIKOVIC
(born May-12-1961, 59 years old) Yugoslavia (federation/nationality Serbia)
[what is this?]
FM (1985); GM (1996).

Strikovic won the 1992 Championship of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. He was runner up at International Opens in Damiano De Odemira in 2005 and in Calvia in 2006, and then won the International Open in Malaga in 2010. He also won the City of Gaia International Tournament and the Pedro Lezcano Montalvo Memorial Tournament, both in 2010.

Strikovic has also won over 40 open tournaments played in Spain over the last 20 years, most of them played in rapid time format. These are listed at the following link: Aleksa Strikovic.

He also lives in Spain.
