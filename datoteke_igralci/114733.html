MARINA ROMANKO NECHAEVA
(born Aug-05-1986, 34 years old) Russia
[what is this?]

Marina Yevgenyevna Romanko Guseva (now Nechaeva) is an IM & WGM. She competed in the FIDE Knock-out Women's World Championship (2012), but lost in the first round to former Women's World Champion, and Bulgarian GM, Antoaneta Stefanova.
