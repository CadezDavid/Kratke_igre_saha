MIKHAIL S TSEITLIN
(born Jun-16-1947, 73 years old) Belarus (federation/nationality Germany)
[what is this?]
Mikhail Semyenovich Tseitlin was awarded the IM title in 1977 and the GM title in 1987. He was Moscow Champion in 1976 (jointly) and 1977 and in tournaments had 1st= placings in Naleczow 1979 and Pernik 1981.
