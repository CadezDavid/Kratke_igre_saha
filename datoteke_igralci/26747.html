ANDREI ANATOLYEVICH OBODCHUK
(born Sep-28-1955, 65 years old) Russia
[what is this?]

International Master (1994); FIDE Trainer (2016). He played for IPCA (= International Physical-Disabled Chess Assocation) in the Olympiads 2002-2014, with the exception of 2004. IPCA's World Champion 2010 and 2014, and runner up in 2013.
