ILDIKO MADL
(born Nov-05-1969, 51 years old) Hungary
[what is this?]

Ildiko Madl was born in Tapolca, Hungary. She was European Women's Junior Champion in 1984 and was awarded the WIM title in the same year. She won the World U-20 Women Championship in 1986. After that she was awarded the WGM title. In 1992 she became an IM. Hungarian women's champion in 1990, 1991 and 1993.
