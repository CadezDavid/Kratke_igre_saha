MARTA I LITINSKAYA-SHUL
(born Mar-25-1949, 71 years old) Ukraine
[what is this?]
Marta Ivanova Litinskaya-Shul was born in Lvov, Ukraine (formerly USSR). Awarded the WIM title in 1972 and the WGM title in 1976, she was USSR Women's Champion in 1972. In 2002, she won the World Senior Women Chess Championship.
