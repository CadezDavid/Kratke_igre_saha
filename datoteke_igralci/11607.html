UWE BOENSCH
(born Oct-15-1958, 62 years old) Germany
[what is this?]
Uwe Bönsch is a (East) German player who was awarded the GM title in 1986. He was either first or first equal in the following tournaments; Leipzig 1986 (Category 8), Budapest Open 1986, Leipzig 1986 (Category 9), Portoroz 1987, and Polanica Zdroj 1987. He is also a FIDE Senior Trainer.
