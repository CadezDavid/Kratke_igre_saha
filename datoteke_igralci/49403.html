  Tabanus: GM Vlad-Cristian Jianu, Romania, born 27 September 1984. Now rated 2527, http://ratings.fide.com/card.phtml?...

IM in 2002. GM in 2007. Rated 2544 in Oct. 2008

Played in World U16 olympiads 1999 (board 3) and 2000 (board 2), and in European Team Ch 2007 (board 3, 4.5/7, perf. 2589).

World U10 vice champion 1994. European U10 vice champion 1994. European U12 vice champion 1996. European Junior Team Champion 2002. Champion of Romania 2006.

Placed 7th in World Junior Ch 2004, 1st in Timisoara Cup 2005 (1st GM norm), 1st in Timisoara Cup 2006 (2nd GM norm), 6th in Bucharest Autumn Cup 2006, 8th in Ciocaltea Memorial 2007, 69th in European Individual Ch 2007 (3rd GM norm), 1st in Galati Open 2007, 5th in Romanian Ch 2008, 4th in Felix Cup 2008, 5th in Ciocaltea Memorial 2009, 3rd in Arad Open 2009.
