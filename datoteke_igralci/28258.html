ZSUZSA VEROCI PETRONIC
(born Feb-19-1949, 71 years old) Hungary
[what is this?]

Zsuzsa Veroci-Petronic was born in Budapest. She was awarded the WIM title in 1969 and the WGM title in 1978. She is also an International Arbiter.
