RUPERT WILLIAM JONES
(born Jun-10-1961, 59 years old) Papua New Guinea
[what is this?]

A FIDE Master and International Arbiter, Rupert Jones represented Botswana at the 1986, 1988, and 1990 Olympiads and Papua New Guinea at the 2004 and 2006 Olympiads.
