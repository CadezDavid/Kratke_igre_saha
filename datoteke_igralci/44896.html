BORIS NIKOLOV CHATALBASHEV
(born Jan-30-1974, 46 years old) Bulgaria (federation/nationality Denmark)
[what is this?]

GM Boris Nikolov Chatalbashev played in the Olympiads of 1996 (2nd reserve), 1998 (1st reserve), and 2004 (1st reserve), and in European Team Championship 2003 (board 3) and 2007 (1st reserve). Four times Bulgarian champion: in 1991, 1998, 2007 and 2010.

Winner of over 30 tournaments. Recent results include 2nd at Bad Liebenzeller (Germany), behind Vladislav Romanovich Borovikov, 2nd in the Albena Open in Bulgaria (2010) and won the 12th South Wales International 2015 outright with 8/10. (1)
