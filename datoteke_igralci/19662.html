EMIR DIZDAREVIC
(born Apr-02-1958, 62 years old) Bosnia and Herzegovina
[what is this?]

Emir Dizdarevic was awarded the GM title in 1988. He was 1st= at Pleven 1987 and 1st= at Sarajevo 1988. He was Bosnian Champion in 2009, 2011 and 2012. Winner of Zenica in 2009.
