SUBBARAMAN VIJAYALAKSHMI
(born Mar-25-1979, 41 years old) India
[what is this?]

Vijayalakshmi is an IM and a WGM. She was Indian women's champion in 1995, 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001 and 2002. She has three GM norms.

She is married to Jha Sriram.
