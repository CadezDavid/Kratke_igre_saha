EKATERINA POLOVNIKOVA-ATALIK
(born Nov-14-1982, 37 years old) Russia (federation/nationality Turkey)
[what is this?]

International Master and Woman Grandmaster.

In April of 2006 she won the 7th European Individual Championship: Women (2006). She was Turkish Women's Champion in 2008, 2016 and 2018. Atalik is also a FIDE Instructor.

She is married to Suat Atalik.
