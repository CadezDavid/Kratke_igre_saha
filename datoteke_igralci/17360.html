ZHAOQIN PENG
(born May-08-1968, 52 years old) China (federation/nationality Netherlands)
[what is this?]
GM & WGM Zhaoqin Peng was born in China in 1968. She left her homeland in 1996 and now lives in the Netherlands, where she is the #1 rated female player. Peng has won the Dutch Women's Championship thirteen times, including a streak of twelve consecutive championships from 2000 through 2011.

In the Dutch Women's Championship (2005) she achieved an incredible 8/10, winning two full points ahead of her nearest rival. This was no fluke: in the Dutch Women's Championship (2007) she finished with 8/9 points, winning once more by two full points. She is also a FIDE Instructor (2010).
