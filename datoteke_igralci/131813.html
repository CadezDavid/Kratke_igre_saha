DAVID ANTON GUIJARRO
(born Jun-23-1995, 25 years old) Spain
[what is this?]

Anton became a FIDE Master in 2009 and an IM in 2012. In 2013 he was awarded the Grandmaster title and in 2014 he came 2nd in the European Individual Championship (2014) with 8/11 behind Alexander Motylev, qualifying for the World Cup (2015), from which he was eliminated when he lost his first round match to Romanian-German GM Liviu Dieter Nisipeanu.
