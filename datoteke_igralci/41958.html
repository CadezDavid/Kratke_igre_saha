BOJAN KURAJICA
(born Nov-15-1947, 72 years old) Croatia
[what is this?]

Bojan Kurajica was born in Ljubljana, Yugoslavia (Slovenia). Awarded the IM title in 1965 and GM title in 1974, he was World Junior Champion in 1965.
