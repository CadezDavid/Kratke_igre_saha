Jun-25-06  wkargel: I had the pleasure of meeting Ms. Blanco this past weekend at a small tournament in Atlanta. She was also an instructor at the Emory Castle Chess Camp in Atlanta a couple of weeks ago.

Based on the kibitzing comments here, I would have to concur that Ms. Blanco has gained a few pounds, but I don't feel that this is a big deal at all...she has not gained that much weight anyway, and (I might be in the minority here) think that it actually adds to her attractiveness...i.e. she is a bit curvier now.

I was planning on taking some photos of some of the top players in action over the board at the tournament (including Ms. Blanco), but I got so emroiled in my own pitched battles over the board that I just forgot to! Perhaps next time!

Ms. Blanco was very nice, and even helped analyize a number of games from the tournament. She in fact was even upset by a player with a much lower rating in the tournament, yet she took it in stride!

I was not able to find out if Ms. Blanco was staying in the USA for an extended period of time or not. Last thing I heard was that she was studying dentistry in Brazil? Perhaps she plans on practicing here in the USA? Regardless of where she ends up, I hope that she will continue to play chess competitavely!
