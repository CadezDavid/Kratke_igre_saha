VIVIAN SMITH
(born Sep-15-1951, 69 years old) New Zealand
[what is this?]

Woman FIDE Master Vivian J Smith is the wife of Robert Wayne Smith. A strong player in her own right, Vivian has won the New Zealand Women's Championship a record ten times.
