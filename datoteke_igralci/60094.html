  biglo: Vojislav Milanovic - born 23 September 1961 in Yugoslavia (now Serbia). He achieved his IM title in 1993 and achieved a GM norm in 1996. In 2005 he qualified as a FIDE approved chess trainer.

He has a record of 2 wins and a draw against Peter Leko. He was a two-time winner of the Belgrade Chess Cup.

He has worked as a chess trainer for the "Partizan" chess club and he has co-written a book on Spanish Gambits with GM Milan Matulovic and translated Nottingham 1936, by Alexander Alekhine. He currently works as a journalist and writes a chess column in Blicpress.
