LELA JAVAKHISHVILI
(born Apr-23-1984, 36 years old) Georgia		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]
She is an IM & WGM. Georgian champion (w) in 2001 and 2007. Made it to the 3rd round of the FIDE Knock-Out Women's World Championship (2012), defeating Russian IM Anastasia Bodnaruk and Ukrainian GM Kateryna Lahno before losing to Indian GM Dronavalli Harika.
