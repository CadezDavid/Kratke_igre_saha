POURIA DARINI
(born 1991) Iran
[what is this?]
IM (2010); GM (2013).

Master norms (1)

<International Master> Darini won his first IM norm at the World U18 Championship in November 2009 and a double IM norm at the 9th Asian Continental Championship that finished on 29 April 2010. (1) He thereby gained his IM title at the age of 19 years.

<Grandmaster> His first GM norm came from the 2012 Asian Nations Chess Cup in May 2012, and his second, a double GM norm, was acquired at the Chess Olympiad (2012), which finished on 9 September 2012. He was 21 years of age at the time.

Championships

Darini won the Iranian Semi Final Chess Championship in 2009. (2) After qualifying through Zonal 3.1, this Grandmaster lost to Dmitry Andreikin at the World Cup (2013) in the 1st round.

Standard Tournaments

Winner of the 9th Ebne Sina Open played in Hamedan, Iran in July 2010. He also won the 4th Sardaran Open 2015 in July 2015. (3)
