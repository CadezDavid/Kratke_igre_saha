ILIA ILJIUSHENOK
(born Aug-16-1993, 27 years old) Russia
[what is this?]

International Master (2013); Grandmaster (2018).

Shot to prominence in March 2015 when the untitled player finished =5th (14th on tiebreak) in the European Individual Championship (2015). This result not only earned him a double GM norm, but by reason of qualifying for the World Cup (2015), he would have received a direct award of the International Master title had he not already won 3 IM norms prior to this result. As he had previously achieved a rating of 2400 without an application for the title having been lodged with FIDE prior to his EICC result, his IM title comes into effect retrospectively from 13 October 2013. (1) He met Dmitry Jakovenko in the first round of the World Cup and lost in the blitz tiebreaker after drawing the standard games and two sets of rapid game tiebreakers.
