ILAHA AKIF QıZı KADIMOVA
(born Nov-05-1975, 45 years old) Azerbaijan
[what is this?]
WGM (1994).

Kadimova became Belarusian Women's Champion in 1990. She was World U18 (Girls) Champion in 1992 and 1993, European Girls Champion (U16 and U20) in 1991 and 1993, USSR U18 (Girls) Champion in 1991 and Student World Champion in 1994.
