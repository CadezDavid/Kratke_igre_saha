KRUM GEORGIEV
(born May-24-1958, 62 years old) Bulgaria
[what is this?]

GM (1988) Krum Georgiev was 1st= at Athens 1983, 2nd= at Albena 1985 and 3rd= at Pleven 1987. In 1986 he played board 5 for the Bulgarian Olympiad team.
