NICLAS HUSCHENBETH
(born Feb-29-1992, 28 years old) Germany
[what is this?]
Niclas Huschenbeth was born in Hamburg and learned how to play chess at the age of 5 or 6 from his father. In 2005 he won the U14 German Chess Championship. He became an IM in 2008 and German champion in 2010. In 2012 he was awarded the Grandmaster title.
