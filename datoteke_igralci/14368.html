ALEKSANDER SZNAPIK
(born Feb-10-1951, 69 years old) Poland
[what is this?]

Aleksander Sznapik was born in Warsaw. Awarded the IM title in 1977, he was Polish Champion in 1976, 1980, 1984 and 1991. Winner of Warsaw 1979.
