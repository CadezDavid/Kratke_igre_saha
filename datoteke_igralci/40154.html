JORGE ALDRETE LOBO
(born Jul-13-1940, 80 years old) Mexico
[what is this?]
International Master and GMC. He learned the game of chess in 1954, at the age of 14, and represented Mexico at the World Junior Championship (1957) in Toronto. He started playing correspondence chess in 1990, at the age of 50.
