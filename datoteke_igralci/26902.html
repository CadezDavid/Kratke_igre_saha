SERGIO SLIPAK
(born Jul-28-1965, 55 years old) Argentina
[what is this?]

International Master (1990); Grandmaster (2001); FIDE Senior Trainer (2015). Sub-champion of Argentina (1998 and 2002) and =1st (5th on tiebreak) at the 2012 Argentine Championship. Won at Mar del Plata in 1995, 1999 and 2001. Placed 2nd at the inaugural Yael Graphics International Master Festival - White Tower in 2010 behind Uruguayan GM Andres Rodriguez Vila.
