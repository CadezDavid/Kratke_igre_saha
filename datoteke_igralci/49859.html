JOANNA DWORAKOWSKA
(born Oct-21-1978, 42 years old) Poland
[what is this?]
Joanna Dworakowska is a Woman Grandmaster and International Master. She won the Polish Women's Championship in 1997, 1998, and 2001. In 1998, she was second in the World U20 Championship for girls.
