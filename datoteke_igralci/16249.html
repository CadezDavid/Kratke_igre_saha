UTUT ADIANTO
(born Mar-16-1965, 55 years old) Indonesia
[what is this?]
Utut Adianto was born in Jakarta. He learned to play chess at the age of six and won the Jakarta Junior Championship at the age of twelve. In 1982, he won the the Indonesian national championship. He was awarded the IM title in 1985 and the GM title in 1986. He was 3rd= at Jakarta in 1986 and 1st= at San Francisco 1987. He is chairman of the Indonesian Chess Association (Percasi).
