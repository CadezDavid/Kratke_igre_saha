JORGE ALBERTO RUBINETTI
(born Mar-31-1945, died Sep-18-2016, 71 years old) Argentina
[what is this?]

Jorge Alberto Rubinetti was awarded the IM title in 1969. He was Argentine champion in 1971-72 and 1982 (after a play-off), 1988 and 1991.
