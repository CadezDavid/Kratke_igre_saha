ELVIRA BAYAKHMETOVNA BEREND
(born Sep-19-1965, 55 years old) Kazakhstan (federation/nationality Luxembourg)
[what is this?]

WGM Elvira Bayakhmetovna Berend won the 2017 Womens' O-50 World Seniors' Championship(1) with a score of 7 points after nine rounds. She finished with 8 points (tied with Tatiana Bogumil but Berend lost on the head-to-head tiebreak of the 8th round) at the 2016 Womens' O-50 World Seniors' Championship(2) in 2016 to capture 2nd place. Her maiden name is Elvira Sakhatova.
