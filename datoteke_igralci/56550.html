MOHAMED AMINE HADDOUCHE
(born Aug-19-1984, 36 years old) Algeria
[what is this?]

He became an IM in 2004 and a Grandmaster in 2014. Champion of Algeria in 2005, 2009, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2017. His Zonal 4.1 title qualified(3) him to the 2017 World Cup.
