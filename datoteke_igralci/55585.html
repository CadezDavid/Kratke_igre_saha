LILI TOTH
(born Feb-02-1986, 34 years old) Hungary
[what is this?]
Dr. Lili Toth is a WIM. She is a multiple medalist of Hungarian youth championships. She reached the 4th place in women championship Visegrád (2008). In 2009 she finished her studies at the law faculty of Károli Gáspár University. Best results: U16 Hungarian Ch. 2001 1st place, U20 Hungarian Ch. 2002, Hungarian Ch. Visegrád 2008 4th place.
