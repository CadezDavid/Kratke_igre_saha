TAMAZ GELASHVILI
(born Apr-08-1978, 42 years old) Georgia
[what is this?]
Tamaz Gelashvili was awarded the Grandmaster title in 1999. He was Georgian champion in 2000 and 2010 (=Merab Gagunashvili). He won the 6th New York International 2013.
