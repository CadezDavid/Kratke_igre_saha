SOPIKO KHUKHASHVILI
(born Jan-04-1985, 35 years old) Georgia
[what is this?]
Khukhashvili is an IM and a WGM. She won the girls' Under-16 section of the World Youth Chess Championship twice, in 1999 and 2000. She contested the FIDE Knock-Out Women's World Championship (2012) but lost in the second set of the first round tiebreaker to Armenian GM Elina Danielian.
