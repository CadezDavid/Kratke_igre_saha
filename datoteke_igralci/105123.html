ANASTASIA BODNARUK
(born Mar-30-1992, 28 years old) Russia		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]
Bodnaruk became a WGM in 2009 and an IM in 2010. She came =1st with 9.5/13 at the World Junior Championship (Girls) (2012), but placed 3rd on tiebreak behind the winner Qi Guo and runner up WGM Nastassia Ziaziulkina. She qualified for the FIDE Knock-out Women's World Championship (2012) but lost the second set of tiebreakers in her first round match with Georgian IM Lela Javakhishvili to exit the title contest. She won the 66th Russian Championship Higher League (Women) (2013) with 7.5/9, a point clear of the field and was =1st at the Satka Autumn 2013..
