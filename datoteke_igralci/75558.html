AMINA MEZIOUD
(born 1988) Algeria
[what is this?]
Mezioud is a WIM who gained her title in 2005 when she won the African Zonal 4.1 (Women). She played in the FIDE Knock-out Women's World Championship (2012) but lost in the first round to Anna Muzychuk.
