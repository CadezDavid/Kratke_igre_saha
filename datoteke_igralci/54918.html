DEIMANTE DAULYTE-CORNETTE
(born Feb-22-1989, 31 years old) Lithuania
[what is this?]

Deimante Cornette, nee Daulyte, is an International Master (and a WGM). She was Lithuanian Women's Champion in 2006, 2007, 2008, 2012 and 2013. She is married to Matthieu Cornette.
