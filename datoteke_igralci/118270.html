GIL POPILSKI
(born Oct-06-1993, 27 years old) Israel
[what is this?]

International Master (2010), Grandmaster (2013), European U16 champion 2009. Won a GM norm at the 13th European Individual Championship (2012) and another at the powerful Isle of Man Masters (2014) where he was a joint runner-up. He tied for share of 2nd-4th with Gata Kamsky and Kayden W Troff at the 2016 Calgary International tournament.
