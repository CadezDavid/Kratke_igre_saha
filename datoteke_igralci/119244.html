DINARA SADUAKASSOVA
(born Oct-31-1996, 24 years old) Kazakhstan
[what is this?]

By 2017, Kazhakstani IM Saduakassova became their top women's player for Kazakhstan. She is also a Women's Grand Master (2012), becoming the 2010 Girls U14 World Champion, the 2014 Girls U18 World Champion (with a round to spare) and the World Junior Girls Champion 2016 (World Junior Championship (Girls) (2016)).
