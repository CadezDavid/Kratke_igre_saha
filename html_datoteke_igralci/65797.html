NAMIG GULIYEV
(born Feb-13-1974, 46 years old) Azerbaijan
[what is this?]
GM Namig Guliyev (Gouliev). With a score of 7.5/11, he placed 19th on tiebreak at the 12th European Individual Championship (2011), qualifying him to participate in the World Cup (2011); however he was beaten in the first round of the Cup by Georgian super-GM Baadur Jobava.
