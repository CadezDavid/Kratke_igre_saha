LENKA PTACNIKOVA
(born Jan-16-1976, 44 years old) Czech Republic (federation/nationality Iceland)
[what is this?]

Ptáčníková is a WGM and a FIDE Trainer. She was Czech Women's Champion in 1994 (Nymburk) and 1996. Icelandic Women's Champion in 2006, 2009, 2010 and 2012-2018. She is married to Omar Salama.
