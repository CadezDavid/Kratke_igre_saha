DUSKO PAVASOVIC
(born Oct-15-1976, 44 years old) Slovenia
[what is this?]

Dusko Pavasovic was born in Split, Croatia, but he plays for Slovenia. He has been a grandmaster since 1999, and has taken part in seven Olympiads. Pavasovic was Slovenian champion in 1999, 2006 and 2007. He is also a FIDE Trainer (2012).
