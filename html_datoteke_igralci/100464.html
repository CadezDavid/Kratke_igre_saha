ERIK BLOMQVIST
(born Oct-19-1990, 30 years old) Sweden
[what is this?]

IM (2008); Grandmaster (2013). Swedish Champion in 2016.

Blomqvist won his GM norms at the World Junior Championship (2010) (8/13), Politiken Cup 2011 (6/9) and at the 4th Borloy Androvitzky Karoly Memorial Tournament, the First Saturday GM event held in July 2013, scoring 6/9. All his scores at these events were the requisite scores for GM norms. (1)

Equal second (seventh on tiebreak) at the Barcelona Open 2015. (2)
