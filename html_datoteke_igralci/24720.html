DRAZEN SERMEK
(born Jan-30-1969, 51 years old) Slovenia (federation/nationality Croatia)

GM Drazen Sermek was Slovenian champion in 1993 and 1998 and a member of the Slovenian Olympic team in 1994, 2000, 2002, 2004 and 2006.

After I made my 41st move I offered my opponent a draw. He thought about for a few seconds and then nodded his agreement. During the game I was considering 27...f5 but was only looking at 28.Bxb7 Rb8. As my powerful opponent was polishing off his partners on the other boards so his appearance at mine became more frequent and I had no time to think. It was only later that it occurred to me that Black can play 28...Rc7. Still I was very pleased to draw this game. Let me know your opinion.
