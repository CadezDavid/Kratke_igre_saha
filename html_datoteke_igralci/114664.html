ANNE HAAST
(born Jul-01-1993, 27 years old) Netherlands
[what is this?]

WIM (2010); WGM (2015). Placed 4th in the European Girls U16 Championship 2009. Dutch Women's Champion in 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017. Dutch Women's Rapid Champion in 2015, scoring 6.5/7, a point clear of the field. She won the Dutch Championships (Women) (2017).
