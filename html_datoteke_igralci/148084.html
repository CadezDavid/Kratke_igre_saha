LAURA UNUK
(born Nov-09-1999, 20 years old) Slovenia
[what is this?]

Woman Grandmaster (2019).

Unuk became Slovenia's top female and top U-18 player by 2017. In 2014, she was awarded both of her Woman IM and Woman FIDE master titles. She won her WIM title(2) when she became the World U16 Girls Champion (2014).
