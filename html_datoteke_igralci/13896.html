PETER K WELLS
(born Apr-17-1965, 55 years old) United Kingdom
[what is this?]

Peter Kenneth Wells was born in Portsmouth, England and became a FIDE Master in 1982, an IM in 1987, and a GM in 1994. He is also a FIDE Senior Trainer (2015).
