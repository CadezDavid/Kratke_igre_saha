YAIR KRAIDMAN
(born Nov-01-1932, 88 years old) Israel
[what is this?]
Yair Kraidman was awarded the IM title in 1964 and the GM title 1976. He played on ten Israeli Olympiad teams between 1958 and 1976 and in tournaments he was 2nd at Netanya 1975 and 1st= at Beer-Sheva 1976.
