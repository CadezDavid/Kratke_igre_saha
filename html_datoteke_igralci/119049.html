GULISKHAN NAKHBAYEVA
(born Jun-20-1991, 29 years old) Kazakhstan
[what is this?]

International Master and Woman Grandmaster. Nakhbayeva became Asian U16 Girls Champion in 2007. In 2010, she scored 5.5/9 at the Moscow Open (section C), with a performance rating of 2348, came 2nd in the Asian Junior (Girls), and scored 8.5/13 in the World Junior Championship (Girls) (2010) for =7th-13th place.
