SEBASTIAN BOGNER
(born Jan-17-1991, 29 years old) Germany (federation/nationality Switzerland)

Sebastian Bogner was born in Pforzheim. He became an IM in 2005 and a GM in 2009. He is also a FIDE Trainer (2015). Swiss Champion in 2018.
