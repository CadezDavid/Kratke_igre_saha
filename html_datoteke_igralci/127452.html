MARINA BRUNELLO
(born Jun-16-1994, 26 years old) Italy
[what is this?]

International Master (2019).

She became an FM in 2014, a WGM in 2016, a WIM in 2010 and a WFM in 2008. She was Italy's 2nd ranked women's player in 2017 and Italian Women's Champion in 2008 and 2018. Sister of Sabino Brunello and Roberta Brunello.
