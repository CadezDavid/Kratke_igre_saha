MARTIN LORENZINI
(born Sep-26-1975, 45 years old) Argentina
[what is this?]

Champion of Argentina (2012); Grandmaster (2014).

Played reserve board for Argentina at the Chess Olympiad (2012) in Istanbul.

Gained his three grandmaster norms at the 11th Anibal International Open in 2005 (in Linares in Spain), the Argentinian Championship 2012 and at the Argentinian Championship of 2014.
