JOUNI YRJOLA
(born Oct-24-1959, 61 years old) Finland
[what is this?]
Jouni Yrjola was born in Vilppula. He was awarded the IM title in 1984 and the GM title in 1990. He was Finnish champion twice, in 1985 and 1988.
