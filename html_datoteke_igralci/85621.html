VAIDYANATHAN RAVIKUMAR
(born Dec-26-1959, 60 years old) India
[what is this?]
Vaidyanathan Ravikumar, a Commerce graduate of Madras University, was awarded the IM title in 1978 after winning the Asian Junior Championship in Tehran. He has represented India at the Olympiads in Malta (1980) and Thessaloniki (1984) and twice participated at the World Junior Championship in Austria (1978) and Norway (1979).

He has authored biographical works on Ulf Andersson, Anatoly Karpov and Jan Timman as well as works on the Caro-Kann and Benko Gambit.
