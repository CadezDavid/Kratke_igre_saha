GU XIAOBING
(born Jul-12-1985, 35 years old) China		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]
Gu Xiaobing is a WGM. Runner-up in the 2005 World U20 Girls Championship, she competed in the FIDE Knock-Out Women's World Championship (2012) but lost in the first round to Russian IM Valentina Gunina.
