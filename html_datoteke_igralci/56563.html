ANUAR ISMAGAMBETOV
(born Mar-21-1986, 34 years old) Kazakhstan
[what is this?]
Grandmaster. Champion of Kazakhstan in 2008 and second in 2007. Best results: Astana championship 2002, 1st; Saratov IM 2004, 1st; Zonal 3.6 championship, 1st; Alushta Summer 2008, 1st; Gurgaon International Open 2009, 1-4th; Pavlovar Open 2009, 4th; Maralsay 2010 (KAZ), 1st; World University championship 2001, 4-6th.

A participant in the 2006, 2008 and 2010 Olympiads and to the 2007 World Cup.
