ANDRAS ADORJAN
(born Mar-31-1950, 70 years old) Hungary		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]

András Adorján was born in Budapest as András Jocha. He adopted his mother's surname Adorján in 1968. Awarded the IM title in 1970 and the GM title in 1973, he was a Candidate in 1980 but was eliminated by Robert Huebner (+1, =7, -2). He was also European Junior Champion in 1969-70 and Hungarian Champion in 1973 (jointly), 1992 and 1993. He finished 2nd behind Anatoly Karpov at the World Junior Championship in 1969 and has won 1st prizes at Budapest 1982, Gjøvik 1983, Esbjerg 1985 and the New York Open 1987. Adorján is also an International Arbiter (1994).
