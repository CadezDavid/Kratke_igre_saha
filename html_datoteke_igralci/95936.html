SRINATH NARAYANAN
(born Feb-14-1994, 26 years old) India
[what is this?]

International Master (2008); Grandmaster (2017).

In late 2002 at the age of eight and a half years, Srinath Narayanam was the youngest FIDE rated chess player in India. In 2005, he finished equal first in the U12 Belfort World Youth Championship alongside Wesley So, Sanan Sjugirov and Samvel Ter-Sahakyan. In 2006, he finished second behind Azerbajani IM Eltaj Safarli in a field of 146 in the 5th Dubai Juniors Chess Championship 2006. In the Hastings Chess Congress (2007), he achieved equal ninth, scoring his first IM norm. Won the 2013 Asian Junior Championship outright with 8/9.
