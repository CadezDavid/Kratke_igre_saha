ZHAO JUN
(born Dec-12-1986, 33 years old) China		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]
Grandmaster (2004).

Born in Jinan, Shandong, China, Zhao Jun is China's 19th Granmdaster. His three GM norms were earned at the 3rd Aeroflot Festival (2004), the 2004 Chinese Team Championship in Jinan and at the World Junior Championship 2004 that was staged in Cochin.

Zhao played in the World Chess Cup (2007) where he defeated Pentala Harikrishna in the first round, but went down to Liviu Dieter Nisipeanu in the second. He placed 2nd at Zonal 3.5 (2014), thereby qualifying for the World Cup (2015) where he lost his first round match to Ian Nepomniachtchi in the rapid game tiebreaker to exit the event.

He is the winner of the Campomanes Memorial Cup 2010, came =1st at the Queenstown Chess Classic (2012) and won the Hastings Chess Congress (2014) with a powerful performance, scoring 8/9.

He placed =5th with 6/11 at the Chinese Championship (2015).
