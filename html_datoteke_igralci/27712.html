GILBERTO HERNANDEZ GUERRERO
(born Feb-04-1970, 50 years old) Mexico
[what is this?]

Grandmaster (1995). He has won the Mexican Championship five times. Gilberto has played 7 Olympiads and with more than 10 years living out of his country, he has won more than 40 international tournaments.(1) He is married to WGM Claudia Noemi Amura of Argentina.
