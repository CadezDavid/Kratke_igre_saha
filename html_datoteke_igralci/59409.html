TATJANA MEFODIYEVNA LEMATSCHKO
(born Mar-16-1948, died May-17-2020, 72 years old) Russia (federation/nationality Switzerland)
[what is this?]

Tatjana Mefodiyevna Lematschko was a Swiss WGM of Russian origin, but competed for Bulgaria in the 1974-1982 period. She was Bulgarian champion (w) in 1974, 1975, 1978, 1979 and 1981, and has been Swiss Women's Champion on ten occasions, the first time in 1984 and the last time in 2010.
