OSCAR DE LA RIVA AGUADO
(born Jul-15-1972, 48 years old) Spain (federation/nationality Andorra)
[what is this?]
International Master (1994); Grandmaster (2004); FIDE Trainer (2011); FIDE Arbiter (2013).

Grandmaster Óscar de la Riva Aguada was born in Barcelona. He was Spanish champion in 2003 and Andorran champion in 2000, 2001, and 2007. He is Andorra's top player and its only grandmaster.
