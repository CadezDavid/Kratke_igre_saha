ROBERTO CIFUENTES PARADA
(born Dec-21-1957, 62 years old) Chile (federation/nationality Spain)
[what is this?]

Roberto Cifuentes Parada was born in Santiago, Chile. Awarded the IM title in 1984 and the GM title in 1991. He was Chilean Champion in 1982, 1983, 1984, 1985 and 1986 and now plays for the Spanish federation. He is also a FIDE Senior Trainer (2016).
