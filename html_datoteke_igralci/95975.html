SARAH HOOLT
(born Apr-30-1988, 32 years old) Germany
[what is this?]

WGM Sarah Hoolt was born in Nordhorn, Germany but grew up in Bad Bentheim, Germany. Her mother Elisabeth is from the Netherlands and taught her the Dutch language. She studies Business Administration at the University of Essen, Germany. Sarah also likes volleyball and plays the oboe.

In 2006 Hoolt won the German U18 (Girls) Youth Championship. She was German Women's Champion in 2011.
