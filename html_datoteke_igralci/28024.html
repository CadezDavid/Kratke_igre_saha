BO JACOBSEN
(born Dec-12-1948, 71 years old) Denmark
[what is this?]

Bo Jacobsen was born in Randers. He was Danish champion in 1976 and finished in a tie for 3rd-9th overall (before tie-breaks were applied), on par with the other GMs with the same score, at the 2009 World Seniors' championship.
