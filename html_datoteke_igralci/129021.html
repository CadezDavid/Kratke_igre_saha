KRISTIAN STUVIK HOLM
(born Feb-16-1998, 22 years old) Norway
[what is this?]

Stuvik became a FIDE Master in 2012 and an International Master in 2015.

He won his IM norms at the Caissa IM New Year 2013 Tournament, the Chess in Kecskemet IM in September 2014 and the CC in Kecskemet February 2015. (1)

Stuvik placed 3rd in the Norwegian Championship in 2015.
