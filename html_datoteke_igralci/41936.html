ROMAN HERNANDEZ
(born Nov-23-1949, 70 years old) Cuba
[what is this?]
Roman Hernandez Onna was born in Santiago, Cuba. Awarded the IM title in 1975 and the GM title in 1978, he was Cuban champion in 1981-82. He also played on several Cuban Olympiad teams between 1970 and 1984. Hernandez is also a FIDE Trainer (2014).
