MIKE IVANOV
(born 1995) Canada
[what is this?]

He went to the 2010 World U-16 team chess championship with a score of 4.5/10 (tying 11th-14th with 3 wins & 3 draws in 10 games), tie-breaking for 12th on 3rd board & seeing the team tie 8th-10th, the Canadians tie-breaking for 10th. In 2011, he tied for 2nd-4th in the Canadian Youth U-16 chess championship with 5/7. At Brazil, he finished with 4/9 in the 2011 U-16 World Youth Chess Championship & also plays for the Willowdale club in Toronto. He finished in a tie for 3rd-7th (before tiebreaks) at the U18 Canadian youth chess championships in 2013.
