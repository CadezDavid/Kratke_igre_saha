SALVADOR GABRIEL DEL RIO ANGELIS
(born Oct-02-1976, 44 years old) Spain
[what is this?]

Salvador Gabriel del Rio de Angelis was awarded the Grandmaster title in 2002. He took 2nd place in the Spanish Championship in 2003 and 2006. Spanish Champion in 2018.
