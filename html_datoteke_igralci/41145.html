DRAGAN BARLOV
(born Jan-30-1957, 63 years old) Yugoslavia (federation/nationality Serbia)
[what is this?]

Dragan Barlov was awarded the GM title in 1986. He was 2nd at Sochi 1985 and won the Yugoslav Championship in 1986. He is also a chess author.
