XAVIER VILA GAZQUEZ
(born Mar-08-1990, 30 years old) Spain
[what is this?]

Xavier Vila Gazquez won the U18 European Youth Championship in 2008. He became a FIDE master in 2005 and an IM in 2007. In 2011 he became a GM.
