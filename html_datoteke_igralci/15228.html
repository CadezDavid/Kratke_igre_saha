MISO CEBALO
(born Feb-06-1945, 75 years old) Yugoslavia (federation/nationality Croatia)
[what is this?]

Miso Cebalo was born in Zagreb, Yugoslavia (Croatia). He was awarded the IM title in 1978 and the GM title in 1985. He won the 2009 World Senior Chess Championship. Cebalo is also a FIDE Senior Trainer.
