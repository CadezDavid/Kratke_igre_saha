MAARTEN SOLLEVELD
(born Feb-05-1979, 41 years old) Netherlands
[what is this?]
Grandmaster (2012). Won the 25th ECI international youth tournament 1998 (18 player Open section), with the perfect score of 9/9, 2 points clear outright 2nd placed Welsh player Andrew Dyce. Solleveld won his GM norms when he scored 8/10 in the 2002-2003 Dutch team competition, 7/9 at the 20th North Sea Cup in 2005, and 7/9 in the 2012 Dutch team Competition.
