YURIY KRYVORUCHKO
(born Dec-19-1986, 33 years old) Ukraine		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]
IM in 2004, GM in 2006. Rated 2628 in July 2008. ICC: Decker

Played in the European Team Ch 2009 (1st reserve), and in European U18 Team Ch 2003 (board 2). Placed 9th in the European Ch 2008. Placed 2nd in Reykjavik Open 2009. Winner of the Paleochora Open (Pelekanos, Greece) 22-29 July 2010 http://www.chess-results.com/tnr363... and =2nd (9th on tiebreak) at the 13th European Individual Championship (2012) with 8/11, the result qualifying him for the World Cup (2013). Won the Ukrainian Championship (2013) with 7.5/11 on tiebreak ahead of Ruslan Ponomariov.

At the World Cup 2013, Kryvoruchko defeated Indian GM Parimarjan Negi in the first round and progressed to the second round where he unexpectedly defeated English GM Michael Adams. However, he was eliminated when he lost to fellow Ukrainian veteran GM and twice-Candidate Vassily Ivanchuk in the third round.
