MANUEL LEON HOYOS
(born Feb-10-1989, 31 years old) Mexico
[what is this?]

Grandmaster (2008) and one of Mexico's top players. Second in the Arctic Chess Challenge 2010. Qualifying through Zonal 2.3 for the World Cup (2011), GM Leon Hoyos lost in the first round to Spanish super-GM Alexey Shirov. Mexican Champion in 2010.
