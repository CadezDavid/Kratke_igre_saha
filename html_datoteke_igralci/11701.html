ANDREI SOKOLOV
(born Mar-20-1963, 57 years old) Russia (federation/nationality France)		 PRONUNCIATION:
[what is this?]

Andrei Yurievich Sokolov was awarded the IM title in 1982 and the GM title in 1984. He became a Candidate in 1985. He was World Junior Champion in 1982, USSR Champion in 1984 [rusbase-1] and joint Moscow Champion in 1981 [rusbase-2] with Valentin Arbakov. In Candidates matches he beat Rafael Vaganian (+4 =4 -0), pipped Artur Yusupov (+4 =7 -3) in 1986 and then met Anatoly Karpov in the Karpov - Sokolov Candidates Final (1987). Sokolov lost this match (+0 =7 -4) and since then he has failed to repeat his earlier successes.

Wikipedia article: Andrei Sokolov
