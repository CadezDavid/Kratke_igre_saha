LASZLO BARCZAY
(born Feb-21-1936, died Apr-07-2016, 80 years old) Hungary
[what is this?]

László Bárczay was awarded the IM title in 1966, the GM title in 1967 and the GMC title in 1979. At his only Olympiad appearance at Havana 1966 he scored a remarkable ten wins and two draws on bottom board. Among his better results in tournaments were 1st= at the Asztalos Memorial 1967, 1st at Polanica Zdroj 1969 and 1st at Astor 1982. He was a major player but one with extremely variable results.
