(born Jun-29-1994, 26 years old) Belgium

Grandmaster (2015).

He became a FIDE master in 2008 and an IM in 2010. Won the Ajuinentoernooi (in Belgium) in 2013 with 8.5/9. He was Belgian champion in 2012, 2013 and 2016.
