DAVORIN KOMLJENOVIC
(born Jul-01-1944, 76 years old) Yugoslavia (federation/nationality Croatia)
[what is this?]

Grandmaster. IM in 1984, GM in 1991. ICC: prettychess. Tournament wins (later years): Villa de Benasque Open 1999 (2645 performance), Pablo Gorbea Memorial 2001, Nice Open 2003 and Sevilla Open 2006. He tied for share of 2nd-3rd with Gennadi Timoshchenko at the 21st World Seniors Championship (2011) behind Vladimir Okhotnik (trailing the top finisher by 1/2 point who led with 9/11).
