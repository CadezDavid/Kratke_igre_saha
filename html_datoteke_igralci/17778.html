ALOYZAS KVEINYS
(born Jul-09-1962, died Jul-26-2018, 56 years old) Lithuania
[what is this?]

IM in 1990, GM in 1992. Champion of Lithuania 1983 (shared), 1986, 2001 (shared), and 2008. Played in the Olympiads 1992 (board 4), 1994 (bd. 3), 1996 (bd. 2), 2000 (bd. 1), 2002 (bd. 4), 2004 (bd. 3), 2006 (bd. 2, +4), and 2008 (bd. 1). In 2010 he won the GM tourn. in Moss, Norway. Won the 30th ChessOrg Schachfestival 2014 in Germany. (1) He was also a FIDE Trainer (2016).

  Tabanus: GM Aloyzas Kveinys, Lithuania, born 9 July 1962. Now rated 2531, http://ratings.fide.com/card.phtml?...

IM in 1990, GM in 1992. Rated 2555 in 1993 (World no. 123). Rated 2555 in 1997, 2565 in July 2004. Chessmetrics has 2593 and World no. 72 in 1989.

He is a team player, incl. in German Bundesliga and for chess club Moss in Norway. Said to be very solid, rarely losing to players < 2650, and often winning against < 2400.

Champion of Lithuania 1983 (shared), 1986, 2001 (shared), and 2008.

Played in the olympiads 1992 (board 4), 1994 (bd. 3), 1996 (bd. 2), 2000 (bd. 1), 2002 (bd. 4), 2004 (bd. 3), 2006 (bd. 2, +4), and 2008 (bd. 1).
