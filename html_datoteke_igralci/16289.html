CARSTEN HOI
(born Jan-16-1957, 63 years old) Denmark
[what is this?]

Carsten Høi was born in Copenhagen. He was awarded the IM title in 1979 and the GM title in 2001. Høi was Danish Champion in 1978, 1986 and 1992.
