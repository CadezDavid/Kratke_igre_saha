  Tabanus: GM Petar Genov, Bulgaria, born 5 April 1970. Now rated 2476, http://ratings.fide.com/card.phtml?...

IM in 1992. GM in Jan. 2003. Rated 2526 in July 2002. FIDE Trainer.

Played in the olympiad 2002 (board 3), and in European Team Ch 1999 (1st reserve) and 2003 (board 1).

Champion of Bulgaria 1993 and 1999.

Tournament wins: Teteven 1989, Albena 1990, Plovdiv 1989, 1991, 1992, Beograd 1997, Kochani (Macedonia) 1998, Varna 1998, Lausanne 2001 (GM norm, leaving behind > 30 GMs), Herzeg Novi Black Mountain 2002, Subotica 2002 (closed tournament, 3rd GM norm), Annecy (France) 2003, Marseille 2004, ...

Married to WIM Lyubka Genova. They have two children.
