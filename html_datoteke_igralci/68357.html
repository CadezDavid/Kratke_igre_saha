FALKO BINDRICH
(born Oct-17-1990, 30 years old) Germany
[what is this?]
Falko Bindrich was born in Zittau, Germany. In 1996 his older brother taught him how to play chess. He became a FIDE master in 2003, an IM in 2006 and a GM in 2007.
