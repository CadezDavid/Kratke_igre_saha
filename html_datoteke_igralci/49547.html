NISHA MOHOTA
(born Oct-13-1980, 40 years old) India
[what is this?]
Nisha Mohota became a WIM in 1995, a WGM in 2003 and an IM in 2011. She won the Indian U-14-girls Championship in 1993 and 1994, and the National Juniors in 1999 and 2000. She became Indian Women's Champion in 2005. In 2014, she qualified for the Indian Women's Championship by winning the National Challengers tournament.
