HEIKKI WESTERINEN
(born Apr-27-1944, 76 years old) Finland
[what is this?]
Heikki Markku Julius Westerinen was born in Helsinki. Awarded the IM title in 1967 and the GM title in 1975. Finnish champion in 1965, 1966, 1968 and 1970.
