DAVID GARCIA ILUNDAIN
(born Apr-02-1971, died Jun-19-2002, 31 years old) Spain
[what is this?]
David García Ilundain was Campeón Juvenil of Catalonia in 1989 and of Spain in 1991. IM in 1992 and GM in 1995. He died of an untreatable brain tumor on the 19th of June, 2002, just 31 years old.
