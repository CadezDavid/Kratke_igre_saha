EMORY TATE
(born Dec-27-1958, died Oct-17-2015, 56 years old) United States of America
[what is this?]

Tate was born in Chicago, Illinois and awarded the International Master title in 2007. While serving in the Air Force, Tate won the Armed Forces Championship five times. Tate earned a reputation as a swashbuckling tactician. He collapsed and died at a chess tournament on October 17, 2015. A biography, "Triple Exclam!!! The Life and Games of Emory Tate, Chess Warrior," by Daaim Shabazz, was published in March, 2017.
