CSABA HORVATH
(born Jun-05-1968, 52 years old) Hungary
[what is this?]

Grandmaster (1993) and International Arbiter (1998), and brother of Grandmaster Jozsef Horvath. He was Hungarian Champion in 1994 and 1998.

Note: there are several other Csaba Horvaths who play chess.
