4....Bb4 and 4....Nd4 are standard, of course, but some pretty good players have tried 4....Bc5. The old main line was 5.Nxe5 Nxe5 6.d4 Bd6 7.f4. See Janowski vs Lasker, 1904

I showed this game to Stockfish and it rated Black nearly a pawn down after 5....Qe7. Black has to be very careful early on in these open games, but it feels like the punishment exceeded the crime here.
