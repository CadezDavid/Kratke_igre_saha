Jan-29-03  ksadler: 5. .. g6 is of course the mainline, but some people nowadays play 5. .. c6 (A Zapata vs Timman, 2002, Judit Polgar vs Short, 2002, Christian Bauer vs Luther, 2002, U Atakisi vs Luther, 2002). Thomas Luther and Nigel Short have been the two main players who have played this line in 2002, and I suppose it is a solid second (or third if you want to count .. Nd7 in there as well) alternative to the .. g6 lines.
Feb-13-03  ksadler: Back to Larsen after 1. e4 Nf6 2. e5 Nd5 3. d4 d6 4. Nf3 dxe5 5. Nxe5, he played a wide variety of things: the dubious 5. .. e6 (Ivkov vs Larsen, 1965, Tal vs Larsen, 1965), the exciting but probably wrong 5. .. Nd7 (Tal vs Larsen, 1965, Fischer vs Larsen, 1966), and then the Kengis Line 5. .. g6 (Yanofsky vs Larsen, 1968, Sune Berg Hansen vs Larsen, 1999, N Borge vs Larsen, 1999), but Larsen never played the .. c6 lines mentioned here, and his arsenal in Alekhine's Defense in the mainlines is probably wider and more varied than any other GM.
